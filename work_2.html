Project Details

Goal

With the popularity of Maroon 5's "Girl Like You" music video; our CBS6 producer Shannon thought we could use the same camera spinning technique in the music video to showcase most of our 11PM talent. After doing some research and finding some great tutorials me along with Shannon were able to execute this promo by having our talent spin on a chair in front of a green screen.
