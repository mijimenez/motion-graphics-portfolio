Project Details

Goal

DCW50 and CBS6 senior producer Danica needed help with graphics for a DCW50 campaign regarding "zombies" people who needed could use some energy in the morning by watching DCW50. I decided to go with a simple 3D text look that matched their brand. This campaign was nominated for an Emmy in 2018.

Roles

Senior Graphic Designer: 3D and Motion Graphics | Andres Jimenez

Senior Producer: Editing | Danica Neddenien

Programs

Cinema 4D, After Effects
