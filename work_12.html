Project Details

Goal

This was one of the first campaigns I worked on for CBS6. The campaign was made to promote our weather personality Nikki-Dee Ray and her Franchise "Nikki-Dee Be Me" where she took up different professions for viewers to experience. The other graphic designer Rob and I were at the shoot to make sure the shots were set up properly for post production. In post production Me and Rob put the elements together to make it seem the different version of Nikki-Dee Ray were in the same room. I also created web and news elements as well as a unique logo for the franchise.
