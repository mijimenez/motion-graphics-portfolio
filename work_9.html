Project Details

Goal

CBS6 producer Brandy needed help with motion graphics for her "CBS6 Soaps" campaign which was building on the soap opera trend at the time to promote our news content. I came up with a look for the typography that matched soap operas and mixed it with our brand. This campaign was nominated for an Emmy in 2018.

Roles

Senior Graphic Designer: 3D and Motion Graphics | Andres Jimenez

Producer: Editing | Brandy Brown

Programs

Cinema 4D, After Effects
