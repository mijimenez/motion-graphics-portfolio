About

As a designer, I have a passion for creating. I attended Virginia Commonwealth University's School of the Arts and majored in Communication Arts where I studied fine arts and digital design. After completing a Graphic Design internship at Media General (now known as Nextar), I was introduced to the industry of broadcast television and motion graphics. This experience was a stepping stone into my current position as an Emmy winning Graphic Artist for the local news:

WTVR CBS6(Apr 2017 - Present)Senior Graphic Designer

Media General(Aug 2014 - Dec 2014), (May 2015 - Mar 2017)Graphic Artist

With an advanced knowledge of design and great computer skills, I am ready to offer creativity, professionalism, and the enthusiasm to help brand identity thrive. Some of my skills include: Traditional Graphic Design, Motion Graphics, 3D Graphics, Illustration, Broadcast Design, and Marketing. Some of the programs I use include Adobe Creative Cloud and Cinema 4D.

I'm also from Ecuador, so I'm fluent in Spanish. I love superhero movies, video games, and popcorn. The most important things in my life are my wife, Madeline, and the freedom to do what I love for a living. These are a few things about me, now let me get to know you!

I provide freelance services so check out my portfolio and let me know what I can do for you.
