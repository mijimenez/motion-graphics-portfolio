Project Details

Goal

Right before the election of the next Governor in Virginia CBS6 wanted to run a special highlighting each candidate and informing the public about the stakes the election held. This was the first special I worked on and it took many hours to create the final look. The special was awarded the "Regional Edward R. Murrow Award".
