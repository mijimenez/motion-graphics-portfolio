Project Details

Goal

This started out as an idea from our CBS6 marketing department that required a lot of planning. Me along with the marking director and the senior producer went out to a large field where we had a helicopter, a fire truck, a magician, and our weather personality Nikki-Dee Ray ready to shoot. My role that day was to assist and make sure that all the shots where properly set up for post production where I had to mask each element and incorporate them seamlessly in the final product. I also added the a cappella group in post.
